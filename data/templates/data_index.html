An Analysis and Visualization of Gym Session data


In this project I scrapped "check in" and "check out" data from my gyms website using BeautifulSoup. After the data was cleaned and resolved of any discrepancies, it was stored in an SQLite Database. A mix of Numpy and Pygal was used to analyse and visualize the data.
